Who We Are

The Bureau Cyber Consultancy was founded to help individuals navigate the ever-evolving digital landscape.

The Bureau is a platform for security researchers and practitioners to enhance their existing skill set or gain news skills and experience. Giving new cyber security researchers and seasoned veterans, a safe space to collaborate, mentor and provide a service back into the cyber industry through reporting on issues of interest.

Security researchers will be able to use this platform to enhance their own profile through the reports created for the bureau and veterans can do the same via reporting and/or providing advice and guidance for budding cyber investigators.

The Bureau can be used on cv's as proof of an agents experience in the industry, further supporting a users endeavors for progression in their careers.

Here our agents can pick and choose what they want to report on. Agents can collaborate on a single assignment or work solo.

Mentors will also be able to post their own assignments for Agents to work on.

Our Mission

We aim to provide insightful, actionable intelligence to help Users develop and learn to safeguard against cyber threats.
